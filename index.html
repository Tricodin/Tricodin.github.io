FAQ

Here's some answers to our most frequently asked questions!

When and Where is CUTC East?

CUTC East will be held on September 15th at Memorial University of Newfoundland in the Engineering Building.

Who Can Attend?

High school, undergraduate and graduate students from all across Newfoundland and Labrador can attend. Buy a ticket for $10 in advance or pay $20 the morning of.

What’s different at CUTC then a hackathon?

At CUTC, it’s not just about hacking. Sure, you can get your hands dirty and create things; however, you also get to learn from the people in the trenches--entrepreneurs, pioneers in technology and the ones who build and maintain the tech we use everyday.

What am I getting for my money?

We’ve been working hard to cut prices as much as possible to make sure cost doesn’t get in your way. You get access to keynotes, workshops, challenges, the mini-events, our social & a delicious lunch and dinner!

What are the costs of attending?

Tickets for students will cost $10 (or $20 the day of!).
